Personal Monoliths

Byzantine of frames
Dusty, as they each portray
Some hurt I wrought with

Words that behave like
Calloused hands to soft cheeks &
Wet eyed phantasms

Blurry shapes of those
Who cared & were betrayed by
A Bedouin tongue

Aging reminders
That apologies don't heal
Forever painted

This is my cellar,
I know not what I'm supposed
To do with these scenes

I come here sometimes
& look upon my works, ye
Mighty & despair

Grimacing at the
Thought of adding to my
Wretched collection

Quiet Hours

Can you outgrow your dog?

See, during the growing days,
sometimes you wake up in the
middle of the night to

barking at empty doorways

when your both young

ever since then you don't sleep none without him

cuz he protects you form ghosts

but you trust a dog with these things

when your young

& then if your real unlucky, you find out

a landlord can outgrow your dog

you learn that its hard to find a home for a cute little monster

so you have to learn to sleep on your own

wondering where your son is

you might fetcha few new tricks

for dealing with a quieter house

for openin the front door without that enthusiastic welcome

what you don't exactly ever get to do though

when your real unlucky

is outgrow thinking about your favorite ghost at the quiet hours of the morning

  • Date: March 2017
